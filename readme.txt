CONTROLS
==============================================================================
Use a s d or mouse/touchscreen to control the skills
Space to navigate
red bar (a) will heal the ally with the lowest hp
blue bar (s) will dazzle an enemy at location
green bar (d) will call down airstrike on the location

==============================================================================
THEME
==============================================================================
SPOILER ALERT! 
The story that runs through the game is all about how faith, and lack thereof,
leads our heroes and villains to where they are. If not for lack of faith, the
game's plotline (short as it is due to cuts in production cost) would not exist
and it is this lack of faith that leads to the game's tragic conclusion.
